User Interface & Ease of Use — Vernacular Writing Issues

How spoken vernacular influences written text — demo of friendly data entry, guidance, and automated transfer.

Problem & Goals

People often write words the way they speak (vernacular), which can cause inconsistent spelling, difficulties in formal contexts, or problems for automated systems. This demo shows (1) a simple data-entry form with clear prompts, and (2) automatic transfer of entries to a database table with a basic "standardization" suggestion.
